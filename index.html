About

Hey, I'm Frenchy and I'm a UX Designer who is learning to be a software engineer! I love writing code and creating content from scratch. I used to work in IT and, while repairing computers and phones, I discovered my love of technology! So, I've been pursuing bigger and better things!

Here's my old brand statement (because, why not?): I am a strong, modern New Yorker with a passion for diversity and learning. As a UX Designer and Researcher with a background in IT, I tend to think both logically and creatively. As a result, aesthetics and attention to detail play major roles in my design style. Formerly a psychology major, I have an empathic understanding of people, which makes me a skillful communicator and drives me to stay user-focused.
